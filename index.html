Filipino Food Recipes

Chicken Adobo

This Filipino dish is very wellknown for the foreigners who have traveled in the Philippines, because it's been famous in social medias and several reviews from the foreigners are positive. This dish is easy to cook, soysauce and a little bit of brown sugar are the main ingredients to achieve the savory of the meat that melts in your mouth.

Beef Kare-Kare

My favorite Filipino food of all times! This interesting taste being a Filipino is one of a kind. Filipino loves salty foods but this Kare-kare dish is different with the peanut sauce, and it doesn't have to be salty. However, we ate this dish with rice and a shrimp paste in the side for balancing the saltiness.

Paksiw

A fish stew simmered in vinegar, garlic, black pepper, and specially ginger to make the taste stronger and to achieve the fragrance. This traditional food recipe really love by the most Filipinos, aside from the delicious taste, it is also practical to have this dish because it could last even outside the fridge. The longer you keep it, the more savory it gets!

Dinuguan(Pork Blood Stew)

It is sounds strange, right? But yes, we cook the pork blood with the pork intestine in it. We usually ate this dish with rice but traditionally, this dish eaten by several Filipinos with the white bao-ban by dipping it in the blood stew. Sounds really new? Worth to try!

Tinola

This chicken soup is really good for your soul because it has lemon grass and ginger that could help your body recovers from the fever and colds. This tasty soup is perfect with rice. Nothing can beat this chicken soup!
